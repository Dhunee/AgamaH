We have thus explained how, by uttering, in combination, the two parts of Dvaya which are reed separately in the s’ruti, one can seek, as refuge or upirya, the two feet of the Lord of Sri who, out of compassion, is bent on helping us and who is prepared to pardon us; one can thus attain the Lord of the lotus.born Lakshmi and in the region of bliss attain faultless service to the Lord) along with the full and perfect enjoyment of the Lord.
